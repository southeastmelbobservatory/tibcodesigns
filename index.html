About Tibco Designs

Made-to-measure cabinetry for renovations, new builds and architecturally designed homes.

Tibco Designs creates cabinets for kitchens, bathrooms and robes made to measure. The team works across a variety of finishes and colours, using quality materials including granite, laminates, marble and modern cabinetry surfaces.

Projects can be coordinated alongside your builder or architect, with support for kitchen planning, colour matching and space-saving ideas. From walk-in robes and built-in furniture to custom storage in all shapes and sizes, Tibco can build almost any type of cabinet.
